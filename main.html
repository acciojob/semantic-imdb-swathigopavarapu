Summary

Inception is a science fiction film about Dom Cobb, a thief who enters the dreams of others to steal secrets. He’s offered a chance at redemption if he can accomplish the impossible: planting an idea into someone’s subconscious. The movie explores dreams, reality, and the power of the human mind.
